Visit Our Closest Events

National Adoption Day

April 30th 10:00

It's a day filled with hope, compassion, and second chances where families open their hearts to pets who are waiting for someone to choose them. Our shelter invites the community to meet our wonderful dogs and cats and learn their stories. Whether you're ready to adopt or simply want to support the cause, it's the perfect day to help an animal start a new chapter.

Pet Talent Show

January 24th 16:00

Join us for our fun and lively Pet Talent Show, where our shelter animals get to shine! From playful tricks to adorable moments, each pet brings their own unique charm to the stage. It's a joyful event that celebrates their personalities and helps them connect with future families. Come cheer them on and maybe even meet your new best friend!

Yoga with Cats

July 6th 12:30

Relax, stretch, and unwind during our peaceful Yoga with Cats session, where adorable shelter cats roam freely as you practice. It's a calming experience that blends gentle movement with the soothing presence of cats looking for loving homes. Join us for a feel good event that supports both your well being and theirs.

Shelter Cleanup Day

September 13th 14:15

Join us for our Shelter Cleanup Day, a community event dedicated to giving our animals a cleaner, brighter space to call home. Together, we'll tidy play areas, refresh kennels, and make the shelter more welcoming for pets and visitors alike. Every helping hand makes a difference. Come support our animals in a meaningful and hands-on way.
